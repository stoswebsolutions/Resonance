Academic Team

A team of India’s finest faculty members is the core of the Resonance experience. Most of our faculty members are IITians/NITians for the PreEngineering Division or Doctors from the top Medical Colleges of India for the Medical Division, CAS/CS/MBAs/Law Graduates for the Commerce & Law Program Division.

All the faculty members are recruited through a rigorous recruitment process that involves scrutiny of applications, written test, demonstration lecture, and personal interview.

Newly recruited faculty members undergo an intensive training program called Faculty Training Program (FTP) where his/her knowledge, attitude, and communication are further sharpened. New faculty members also attend the classes of senior faculty members to understand the teaching methodology, effective use of teaching aids, and general behavior in the classroom.
